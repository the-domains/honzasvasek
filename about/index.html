Honza Svasek / inviting life to move in ways it has never moved before

about

Honza researches Butoh since 2007. He worked with some famous masters like: Itto Morita, Atsushi Takenouchi, Ken Mai, Yumiko Yoshioka, Yuko Ota, Imre Thormann, Iwana Masaki, Rhizome Lee, Yukio Waguri and Natsu Nakajima. He studied at the Himalaya Subbody Butoh School since 2011, and graduated spring 2016 after spending two years full time training.

Like his main teacher Rhizome Lee, Honza Svasek approaches Butoh as a Shamanic Performance Art.
